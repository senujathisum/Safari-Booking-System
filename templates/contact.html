At Yala National Park, your adventure fuels our mission. A dedicated portion of every single safari ticket is directly channeled into our frontline conservation programs. From funding anti-poaching patrols and providing vital veterinary care for injured animals, to expanding crucial habitat restoration projects—your visit empowers us to protect and nurture Sri Lanka's incredible biodiversity for generations to come. Thank you for being a guardian of the wild.
